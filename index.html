A Castle in Switzerland

My time in Switzerland was one of the most peaceful and beautiful times of my life. There's something about the place that is just magical. One memory I remember in particular was my time at the castle of Chateau de Chillion. It sits by the most beautiful, clear lake and is surrounded by mountains. When I visited, two women were peacefully swimming across the lake, and I remember being really tempted to just jump in and do the same.

The Day I Return

During my time sitting by the dock, I promised myself that one day, I would return and take a swim in this exact lake like those ladies did. Of course, I would have to learn how to swim first, but if not, I'll use a floaty. I fully intend to live in Switzerland for a few years when I'm older and can afford it.
